Coloring Books


During the lockdown of Covid-19 Pandemic, lots of children were quarantined. Those days were extremely boring and stressing for lots of families... luckily for them the creative community started a trend: coloring books! I jumped on the trend train and quickly put together this coloring books.

Coloring books are widely used in schooling for children for various reasons. For example, children are often more interested in coloring books rather than using other learning methods; pictures may also be more memorable than simply words. Coloring may also increase creativity in painting, according to some research. One of the appeals of adult coloring books is that they help users relax and de-stress.
