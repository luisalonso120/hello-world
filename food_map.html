Hong Kong food map


This map was a collaboration with the social media team. The goal was to promote some of the best restaurants in Hong Kong. The team provided the restaurants and I did the illustrations, art direction and research (locations, Hong Kong objects and maps).

As this escalator system is pretty complex, I tried to simplify it as much as possible. Using our brand colors for the illustrations I added some shadows to give the map some depth.
